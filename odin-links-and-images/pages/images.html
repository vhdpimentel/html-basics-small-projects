Images

This page includes different image formats

JPGs

JPGs are good for photos, as they have good colors and are light, but they don't offer transparent pixels

GIFs

GIF images are good for simple animations, but don't have a limited color palette

PNGs

PNG images are good for icons, logos, etc, since they offer transparent pixels and have a varied color palette

SVGs

SVG images are made of vectors instead of pixels, so they can be scaled up or down depending on the device.
